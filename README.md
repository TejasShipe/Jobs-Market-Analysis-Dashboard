# Jobs Market Analysis Dashboard — Power BI

*Project Overview* -

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing the global Computer Science job market using real-world job listing data. 
The dashboard provides deep insights into job counts, salary distributions, work-from-home trends, hiring platforms, 
and job types — with a drill-through feature to explore any job title in detail.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*Dashboard Pages* -

Page 1 — Jobs Overview -

A high-level summary of the entire CS job market:

Job Count — 479K total job listings analyzed

Avg Job Rating — Star rating visual across all listings

Median Yearly Salary — $113K across all CS roles

Median Hourly Salary — $47.62 across all CS roles

Jobs Over Time — Line chart showing monthly job posting trends (Jan–Dec 2024) with a range slider

Hourly vs Yearly Salary — Scatter plot comparing median hourly and yearly salary per job title

Job Count by Title — Horizontal bar chart ranking all job titles by listing volume

Job Stats Table — Summary table showing job count, median yearly salary, median hourly salary, and sparkline trend per job title


Page 2 — Drill Through by Job Title -

A dedicated detail page that activates when you drill through any job title from Page 1:

Dynamic Title — Page header updates to show selected job title

Yearly Salary Gauge — Min, max, and median yearly salary for that role

Hourly Salary Gauge — Min, max, and median hourly salary for that role

WFH % — Percentage of listings offering work from home

No Degree Mention % — Percentage of listings with no degree requirement

Health Insurance % — Percentage of listings offering health insurance

Global Job Map — Bing Maps visual showing where jobs are located worldwide

Platform Distribution — Bar chart of which platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, BeBee etc.) have most listings for that role

Job Type Breakdown — Full-time vs Contract vs Internship vs Part-time split
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*Key Insights* -

-> Data Engineer is the most in-demand CS role with 129K listings — outnumbering Data Analysts and Data Scientists

-> Machine Learning Engineers command the highest hourly salary at $61/hr despite having fewer listings (13K)

-> Senior Data Scientists earn the highest yearly salary at $156K median

-> Data Analysts have the lowest median hourly salary at $33/hr — showing the entry-level saturation

-> 88% of Data Analyst jobs are full-time positions — stable but competitive

-> Only 10% of Data Analyst roles offer work from home — remote work is rarer than expected

-> 39% of listings do not mention a degree requirement — skills-based hiring is growing

-> LinkedIn dominates job listings across all CS roles followed by BeBee and Indeed

-> Job postings dipped in Sep–Oct 2024 before recovering in Nov–Dec 2024
